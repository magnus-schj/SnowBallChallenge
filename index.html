About

Did you know?

If we harnessed all of the sunlight that reached earth for halv an hour, we would have enough energy to sustain the whole world for a year. That's a lot of energy!

What place would be the best for extracting sunlight? Well, somewhere that wouldnt be in the way for people, and with a stable amount of sunlight. What better place than the dessert? A place with intense sunlight, all year around. Therefore, we have started installing solar panels around the Sahara dessert, generating several renewable terrawatt-hours of energy!
